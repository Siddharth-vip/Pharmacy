Login and My Account

Can I reactivate my inactive account?

Yes, you can reactivate your inactive account by logging in and following the instructions sent to your registered email.

Do I need to verify my mobile number or email every time I log in?

No, you only need to verify your mobile number or email once when setting up your account.

What is an OTP or verification code?

OTP (One Time Password) is a code sent to your registered mobile or email for verification purposes.

How can I use my mobile number to log in?

You can use your mobile number by selecting the "Log in with mobile number" option on the login page.
